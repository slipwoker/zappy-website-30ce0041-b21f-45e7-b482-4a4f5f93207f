Services

From initial concept through final installation, we provide comprehensive support at every stage—ensuring your space receives art that resonates, fits perfectly, and arrives on schedule.

Curation & Sourcing

We begin with discovery—understanding your vision, budget parameters, and spatial requirements. Our team identifies artists and works that align with your aesthetic, whether you seek photo based art, contemporary installations, or classic pieces that serve as visual anchors.

Space Planning

Placement logic matters. We conduct scale studies to ensure each piece commands the right presence—considering sightlines, lighting conditions, material compatibility, and architectural features. Our custom art installation for hospitality spaces balances impact with context.

Installation Management

Coordination, site readiness, and final walkthrough—we handle the logistics so you don't have to. Studio 242 manages timelines, vendor communication, and on-site execution, delivering visual anchor walls commercial spaces can rely on for years to come.

A Clear Process

We believe in predictable milestones and transparent communication. From initial discovery to final installation, every phase is structured to give you confidence and clarity.

Discovery

We begin by understanding your goals, site context, and any constraints. This collaborative conversation ensures we align on vision, budget, and timeline before moving forward.

Selection

We present curated options tailored to your space, complete with scale checks and digital mockups. You review, provide feedback, and approve the final selections with full confidence.

Production & Logistics

Once approved, we coordinate lead times, production schedules, and delivery logistics. You'll receive regular updates and a clear timeline for when your custom art installation will arrive.

Installation & Handover

Our team handles on-site execution with precision and care. We ensure every piece is perfectly positioned, and provide you with care guidance to maintain your visual anchor walls for years to come.
